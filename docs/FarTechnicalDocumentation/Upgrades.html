Upgrades¶

Upgrades are items which can be found as loot on the game map. They provide permanent perks to the shooter object as long as they remain within their inventory.

Lore¶

While fighting in this futuristic waste-land, you notice some gear left from those who have fallen before you. Might be handy to take these with you…

(it’s not stealing if they’re already dead, right?)

Types of Upgrades¶

Name

Bonuses

Armor

Gives shooter 20% damage reduction.

Movement Boots

Max speed is doubled.

Backpack

Consumables slot-type is increased by one.
