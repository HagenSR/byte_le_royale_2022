How it works¶

The loot generation controller handles the spawning of loot around the game map. Money has a 60% chance to spawn, guns 20% and upgrades 20%. Loot is distributed according to a standard distribution, centered at the middle of the game map. Before the spawning of a new loot wave, all of the loot will be removed. Allowing for higher-tier loot to appear as the game progresses.
